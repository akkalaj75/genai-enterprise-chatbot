Benefits Information
====================

**Health Insurance**
- Medical, dental, vision coverage
- Coverage starts on day 1 of employment
- Company pays 80% of premiums
- Employee pays 20%

**401(k) Retirement Plan**
- Company matches 100% up to 4% of salary
- Vesting schedule: 25% per year
- Early withdrawal penalties apply

**Professional Development**
- Annual training budget: $2,000 per employee
- Conference attendance supported
- Online courses reimbursed
- Internal mentorship program available

**Wellness Program**
- Gym membership subsidies
- Mental health counseling (free)
- Wellness challenges with incentives
- Nutrition consultation (free)
