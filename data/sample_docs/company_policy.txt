Company Policy Documentation
============================

This is a sample document used for RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) testing.

**Remote Work Policy**
- Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must maintain core hours: 10 AM - 3 PM EST
- Weekly team meetings are mandatory and in-person

**Leave Policy**
- Annual leave: 20 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Maternity leave: 6 months
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks

**Code of Conduct**
- Maintain professional communication at all times
- Respect diversity and inclusion
- Report violations to HR within 48 hours

**Expense Policy**
- Travel expenses must be pre-approved
- Hotel: Max $200/night
- Meals: Max $50/day
- Submit receipts within 30 days
